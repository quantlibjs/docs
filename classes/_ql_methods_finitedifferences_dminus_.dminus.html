Class DMinus

$ D_{-} $ matricial representation

The differential operator $ D_{-} $ discretizes the first derivative with the first-order formula $$ \frac{\partial u_{i}}{\partial x} \approx \frac{u_{i}-u_{i-1}}{h} = D_{-} u_{i} $$
